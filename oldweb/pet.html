CHAMALEON

I have two chamaleons: Pascual and Pascuala. They are two years old. They are known as veiled chamaleon, Chamaleon Yemen or Chamaleon calyptratus. When she arrived home, Pascuala had a serious lack of calcium and she could not even climb a small step. One year and a half later she was climbing trees!

TORTOISE

Turtles were a birthday gift. They are ten years old. They lived in a small aquarium located in the terrace for four years. When they grew up, we built a lake in the garden where they have been living until today eating snails and other insects.
