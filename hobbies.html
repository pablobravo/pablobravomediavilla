SKIING

My first skiing adventure was in Pajares, Asturias. It was a memorable snowy day on the mountainous slopes in the company of my friend Sergio Rojas. It has since become one of my favorite outdoor activities and I take every opportunity I can to return to that beautiful place.

FOOTBALL

Football has been a part of my life since I was very young. I began playing it for SD Colloto. I played three years there and presently play for a first division futsal team of the University I attend.

GOLF
